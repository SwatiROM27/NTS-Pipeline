## What is Plesk

**[Plesk](https://www.plesk.com/?utm_campaign=plesk_server_default_page_js)** is a hosting [control panel](https://www.plesk.com/blog/business-industry/whats-control-panel-all-you-need-to-know/?utm_campaign=plesk_server_default_page_js) with simple and secure web server, website and web apps management tools. It is specially designed to help web professionals manage web, DNS, mail and other services through a comprehensive and user-friendly GUI. Plesk is about intelligently managing servers, apps, websites and hosting businesses, on both traditional and cloud hosting.
